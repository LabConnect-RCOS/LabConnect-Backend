# Undergraduate Research Program (URP)

## Earning Credits

### Overview
Participating in the Undergraduate Research Program (URP) offers you the opportunity to earn credits while engaging in academic research under the guidance of a faculty sponsor. The number of credit hours earned is flexible, typically ranging from 1 to 4, and is subject to negotiation between you and your faculty sponsor.

### Requirements
To pursue credits through the URP, you and your faculty sponsor must:

- Determine the number of credit hours (1 to 4) to be earned.
- Establish clear expectations regarding your commitment, including weekly time allocation (not exceeding 12 hours) and the nature of required submissions.
- Define the criteria for assessing your performance and assigning a grade.

For detailed information on credit hours and billing, please consult (http://finance.rpi.edu/update.do?artcenterkey=79).

## Applying for Credit

To initiate the credit application process, complete the **4UR Form** in collaboration with your sponsor. Submit the form through the Registrar’s website under **Registrar Requests**, ensuring to copy (CC) your department's URP Coordinator.

### Submission Deadline
Applications for the fall term of 2023 must be submitted by Monday, September 11th, 2023, aligning with the Registrar's "Add Course" deadline.

# Funding Opportunities

## Sponsor-Based Funding

### Overview
Financial support for your research endeavor is typically provided by your faculty sponsor or department. The URP application must be directed to the URP Coordinator within your respective department.

### Responsibilities of the URP Coordinator
The URP Coordinator is tasked with:

- Reviewing the completeness of URP applications.
- Facilitating payroll processing.
- Forwarding approved applications to the Office of Undergraduate Education for final approval.
- Establishing a reporting schedule for tracking hours via web-time entry.

## Matching Funds from the Office of Undergraduate Education

### Overview
The Office of Undergraduate Education offers matching funds of up to $500 per semester to eligible URP participants. However, applications lacking funding from the sponsoring department will not qualify for matching funds. Additionally, late submissions beyond the application deadline will not be considered for matching funds, although departmental funding will still be approved.

## Applying for Funding

To secure funding for your research project:

- Complete the **URP Application for Funding** and secure the necessary signatures.
- Email the completed application to the designated URP Coordinator. In instances where obtaining signatures is not feasible, confirmation from the faculty research supervisor via email will suffice.

### Submission Deadline
Applications for fall 2023 funding must be submitted by Monday, September 11th, 2023, in alignment with the Registrar's "Add Course" deadline.

## Payment Procedures

### Compensation Details
You and your sponsor will determine the total compensation and pay rate. While the minimum hourly rate for URP is $14.20, the actual rate may vary based on negotiations with the sponsoring faculty member. Typically, research commitments entail working eight hours per week, not exceeding twelve hours.

### Payment Schedule
Paychecks are issued bi-weekly, ensuring timely compensation for your research contributions.

For additional information on the Undergraduate Research Program, visit (https://info.rpi.edu/undergraduate-research/undergraduate-research-program-academic-year/).
