ABOUT: The Center for Infrastructure, Transportation, and the Environment (CITE) was established by Rensselaer as a 
collaborative environment in which interdisciplinary transportation and infrastructure-related research could be 
conducted. The center is known more generally for research in sustainable freight system, and freight transportation
planning; transportation planning, design and operations; modeling, and simulation; disaster response logistics; 
traffic signal system control; and behavior research.
